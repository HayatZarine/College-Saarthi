Welcome to College Saarthi

College Saarthi is a platform for students to learn and grow.This is designed for Engineering college students who come from middle class families who have a financial burden or who are unable to afford costly skill courses and education. We have a collection of books, videos, and other resources to help students learn new skills and concepts. We provide a free library, book recommendation and todo list along with academic resources and skill courses.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to provide college students with the resources they need to succeed in their academic journey. We believe that every student has the potential to achieve great things, and we are here to help them reach their goals. Whether you need help finding the right book to read or want to learn a new skill, College Saarthi is here to support you every step of the way. Join us today and start your journey to success!
